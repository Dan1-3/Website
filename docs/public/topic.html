Main objectives of the project

  • Understand how to control and identify use of fake news
  • Awareness about consequences
  • Ethical view

Fake news: non legitimate information

  1. Mis-information: false information with NO bad intentions
  2. Dis-information: false information created to harm:
    • A person
    • Social group
    • Organization
    • Country
  3. Mal-information: information based on reality used to harm

Deepfake

    Characteristics:

  • Image and video manipulation using AI
  • Specific algorithms required (Neural network)
  • Deepfake images: accessible thanks to deepfake software and apps

    Identification:

  • Detection algorithms
  • Media Authentication
  • Technology Platforms
